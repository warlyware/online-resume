I am a copy-editor, front-end web developer, and graphic designer currently working in academic administration at the Center for Inflammation and Mucosal Immunology (CIMI) at the University of Florida. During my time working for CIMI I have edited a wide array of scientific papers including multiple grant proposals which have won significant extramural funding, and manuscripts which have been published in high impact scientific journals, including The Journal of Infectious Diseases (Oxford Journals), Trends in Molecular Medicine (Cell Press), FEBS Letters (Elsevier), and PLOS ONE (PLOS). I produce all of the promotional materials for CIMI, maintain CIMI's WordPress webpage, and oversee the center's annual retreat.
